What is OpenLoop Network

OpenLoop Network is a decentralized wireless network designed to enhance Internet service delivery.

Through its innovative protocol and hardware, property owners and residents can share their Internet bandwidth with the network to earn rewards

OpenLoop Network's decentralized framework uses proof of backhaul, location, and frequency to ensure a secure, trustless system, empowering users and building a robust infrastructure for major AI partners’ data needs

Benefits of Node Validators

With only 25,500 limited nodes, they earn passive rewards for sharing idle bandwidth while holding 40% of $OPL governance power — shaping the ecosystem's future. This dual role offers both financial returns and influence over decision-making.

Each node purchase grants an enhanced OpenLoop points (pts) multiplier, significantly increasing the rate at which points are earned. These points accumulate and can be converted into $OPL, enhancing long-term profitability.

More Details

Rewarding Referral System

OpenLoop provides an opportunity for KOLs, affiliates, and community ambassadors to participate in its referral program, earning commissions for every successful referral. Validators can earn up to 5% cashback when their referred friends take part in the network.

The commission rate is dynamic, increasing as successful referrals grow. This tiered system rewards active participants, granting them higher earning potential as they contribute to the expansion of the Sentry Node Validator Key network.
